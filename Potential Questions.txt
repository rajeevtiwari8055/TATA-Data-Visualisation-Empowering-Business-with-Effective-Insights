✅ Potential Questions for the CEO (Strategic + Revenue Focus)

1️⃣ What is the total revenue, and which countries contribute the most to overall revenue?
2️⃣ Is there any seasonality in sales? (Which months show the highest and lowest revenue?)
3️⃣ Which top 10 products generate the largest share of revenue?
4️⃣ What is the impact of returns (negative quantity / cancelled invoices) on overall revenue?
5️⃣ Is the company’s revenue dependent on a limited number of customers? (Customer concentration risk)
6️⃣ After the United Kingdom, which country shows the highest potential for business expansion?

✅ Potential Questions for the CMO (Customer + Marketing Focus)

1️⃣ What percentage of customers are repeat customers versus one-time customers?
2️⃣ From which countries do high-value customers (top 20% by monetary value) come?
3️⃣ What is the Average Order Value (AOV), and is it increasing over time?
4️⃣ Which products are frequently purchased together? (Cross-selling opportunities)
5️⃣ What is the customer retention rate?
6️⃣ Does customer buying behavior change during specific periods (e.g., festive or seasonal times)?
