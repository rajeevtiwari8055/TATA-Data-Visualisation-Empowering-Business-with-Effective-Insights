## <span id="project-overview">📌 Project Overview</span>

This repository showcases my work from the **“Data Visualisation: Empowering Business with Effective Insights”** simulation offered by **Tata** on **Forage**.  

The program is designed to provide practical exposure in **data analysis, visualization, and communicating actionable insights** to senior management. Participants are tasked with analyzing online retail data to understand key business drivers and support executive decision-making.  

This simulation emphasizes **turning raw data into meaningful insights** to inform operational strategies and marketing decisions.

## <span id="tasks-overview">🧩 Tasks Overview</span>

The program is structured into **four core tasks**:  

1. **Framing the Business Scenario** – Understanding CEO & CMO priorities  
2. **Choosing the Right Visuals** – Mapping data to optimal visualization types  
3. **Creating Effective Visuals** – Cleaning data and designing actionable dashboards  
4. **Communicating Insights & Analysis** – Presenting findings to executives  

---

## <span id="task-1">📌 Task 1: Framing the Business Scenario</span>

**Objective:** Prepare questions relevant to CEO and CMO priorities.  

**Scenario:** An online retail store hired you to review data and provide insights. The CEO and CMO are focused on **revenue drivers, operational efficiency, marketing impact, and expansion opportunities**.  

**Actions Taken:**  

- Reviewed dataset to understand available metrics and demographic information  
- Drafted questions for executive priorities, considering both **quantitative and qualitative** aspects  

**Example Questions**  

**Questions for CEO:**  

1. Which region is generating the highest/lowest revenue?  
2. What is the monthly revenue trend? Which months had the largest increase or decrease?  
3. Are there seasonal patterns in revenue that we should anticipate?  
4. Who are the top customers, and how dependent is the business on them?  

**Questions for CMO:**  

1. What percentage of customers are repeat buyers, and what products are they purchasing?  
2. How frequently do repeat customers place new orders?  
3. What revenue is being generated by repeat customers?  
4. Which customers are repeating the most, and what is their contribution to revenue?  

---

## <span id="task-2">📊 Task 2: Choosing the Right Visuals</span>

**Objective:** Identify visuals that effectively communicate insights to the CEO and CMO.  

**Scenario:** Executives requested visuals to analyze **revenue trends, customer behavior, and expansion opportunities**. Selecting the right chart is critical to avoid misinterpretation.  

**Approach:**  

- Reviewed visualization best practices (**Grammar of Graphics, dimensional mapping**)  
- Selected visuals based on **clarity, impact, and executive requirements**  
- Completed multiple-choice quiz to validate visualization choices  

**Examples of Visualization Decisions:**  

- Time series line chart for monthly revenue trends  
- Bar chart for top 10 revenue-generating countries (excluding UK)  
- Pareto-style descending chart for top 10 customers by revenue  
- Heatmap / treemap for product demand across countries  

---

## <span id="task-3">📈 Task 3: Creating Effective Visuals</span>

**Objective:** Clean the data and develop dashboards in Tableau / Power BI.  

**Actions Taken:**  

- **Data Cleanup:**  
  - Excluded negative quantity returns  
  - Removed incorrect unit prices (below $0)  
  - Applied conditional formulas to filter invalid data  
- Created visuals for each executive question on separate tabs  

**Key Visuals Created:**  

1. **Monthly Revenue (2011)** – Line chart showing seasonal trends  
2. **Top 10 Countries by Revenue & Quantity** – Bar chart excluding UK  
3. **Top 10 Customers by Revenue** – Pareto descending chart  
4. **Product Demand by Region** – Treemap for expansion insights  

---

## <span id="task-4">📝 Task 4: Communicating Insights & Analysis</span>

**Objective:** Present findings to CEO & CMO using insights from the visuals.  

**Approach:**  

- Prepared a **script covering data loading, cleanup, analysis, and insights**  
- Focused on **actionable insights for expansion and revenue growth**  
- Delivered a **5-minute presentation/video** to highlight key trends and recommendations  

**Key Takeaways:**  

- Seasonal revenue fluctuations highlight **opportunity for marketing campaigns**  
- Top-performing regions and customers inform **expansion strategy**  
- Repeat customers are critical for revenue growth, guiding **targeted marketing initiatives**  

---

## <span id="dashboard-development">📊 Dashboard Development</span>

**Objective:** Develop a comprehensive dashboard to showcase insights for executives.  

**Approach:**  

- Used **Power BI / Tableau** to integrate all key metrics  
- Visualized revenue trends, top customers, and country-wise product demand  
- Designed dashboard for **quick executive consumption**, enabling decision-making  
- Created interactive elements for filtering by **month, country, and customer segment**  

**Dashboard Highlights:**  

- **Revenue Trends:** Line charts with monthly granularity for 2011  
- **Customer Insights:** Top 10 revenue-generating customers in descending order  
- **Geographical Insights:** Treemap and bar charts showing revenue and demand across countries  
- **Actionable Filters:** Drill-down for repeat vs. one-time customers
